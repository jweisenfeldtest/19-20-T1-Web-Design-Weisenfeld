Photo Gallery: Dachshunds

This is a Dachsund. Also known as a Weiner Dog, they as typically long in body length, but short in stature.

They were originally bred in Germany to hunt badgers. The name Dachsund actually roughly translates into badger hunter in German.

An average dachshund liter can range anywhere from one puppy to six.

Dachshunds are well known for being quick on their feet. In short bursts an average dachsund can reach 20 mph.

Dachshunds are a very diverse breed as they come in a variety of fur colors and some are even capable of having a long coat.
